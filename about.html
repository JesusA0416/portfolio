Hello! My name is Jesus (yes, like the bible, but not like that). I'm 19, Mexican, and somehow enrolled in two colleges at once. I study Justice Studies, Political Science, and sometimes I pretend I have free time. I am apart of the Salt Lake Community College Honors Programs and the Westminster University Honors College! (NERDDDD, I get it..)


I’m a student and a healthcare worker — rough, I know. I work for Intermountain Health at Primary Children’s Hospital as a Pediatric Behavioral Health Specialist. Which means I spend my days with kids and adolescents (some literally my age) going through it, and somehow still have the energy to write essays at night. I'm also pre-law and pre-nursing, because apparently I enjoy suffering.


I built this site myself. I write code, write papers, and occasionally write emails that start with “Apologies for the delay.” Most of my work lives here because Canvas deletes everything and Google Docs is better than Microsoft Word. Yes, I said it.


Outside of school and work? I’m here — because if I sit still too long, my Mexican parents will ask me to vacuum the roof.


If you're here to read, great.
